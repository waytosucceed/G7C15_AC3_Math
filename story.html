Once upon a time in a magical land, there were two kingdoms, Kingdom A and Kingdom B, separated by a vast forest. The only way to travel between the two kingdoms was through a narrow road that cut through the forest. This road was known as the Transversal Road.

Along this road, travellers encountered various intersections where different paths crossed the Transversal Road at different angles. These intersections played a crucial role in the journey between the two kingdoms. They need to answer the specific questions correctly to reach the kingdom.

The path opened, and they joyously entered Kingdom B, celebrated for their wisdom. The Transversal Road became a symbol of knowledge and unity between the two kingdoms.
